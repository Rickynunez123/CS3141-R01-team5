What is Smartments?

What do we do?

We provide an online portal to make renting more efficient, benefiting both landlords and tenants. We provide a web app in which the tenant will be charged every month using a payment method of their choosing. With that, we will make sure the landlord gets the money on time, and can easily track which tenants have paid

Learn More

How do we do it?

We will send an email notification to the tenant one day before the payment is made. In case their payment does not go through we will notify them every day. If they do not pay the landlord will have the option to send an eviction notice. The result is that the tenant will be more responsible and they will prioritise having the money to pay the monthly rent and we will minimise the interaction between the landlord and the tenant.
